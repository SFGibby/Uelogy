## Symptom
Rep running a CA deal in Enerflo cannot finalize a proposal or send documents — license-related error. Different from IL in that CA requires the individual rep to have an HIS (Home Improvement Salesperson) registration number, not just the company license.

## Likely Cause
- CA law requires the individual salesperson to hold a current HIS number. Enerflo enforces this on CA deals.
- A rep joining mid-cycle may not have their HIS on file in Enerflo yet — pipeline deals will fail until it is added.

## Resolution
1. Ask the rep for their HIS number (they can look up their own via the CA CSLB website).
2. If the rep doesn't have one yet, they cannot close CA deals — route the deal to a rep who does, or hold until registration completes.
3. If the rep HAS an HIS but Enerflo still errors, check their profile in Enerflo V2 settings and make sure the HIS field is populated.
4. Per CCR 2026-03-01 thread, there is a CA HIS lookup tool reps can use for teammates on vacation (<!-- TODO: Sam to grab the exact URL from later transcript sections -->).
