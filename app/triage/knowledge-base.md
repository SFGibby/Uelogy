# SunPower Sales Appointment Knowledge Base

> High-level cheatsheet. Long-tail platform/integration issues live in `brain/<category>/*.md` and are loaded after this file.

## Product lines

- **Direct sales** — SunPower-branded installs sold by our in-house reps.
- **EPC / Field sales** — sold under SunPower umbrella by partnered Engineering, Procurement & Construction firms and installers.
- **Preferred Installer Program (PIP)** — separate partner tier with its own ops contact.

## Deal-stage cheatsheet

- **In-home / mid-pitch** — rep needs fast, confident answer; prefer a 1-sentence response + "happy to deep-dive after."
- **Close / signing** — highest urgency; blockers here go to Sales Ops immediately.
- **Post-contract, pre-install** — usually scheduling or finance questions; not urgent unless cancellation threat.
- **Post-install** — support territory; route to the right CSM team based on product line.

## Urgency rubric (the bot uses this — do not weaken it)

**URGENT** requires at least one of:
- Customer is actively threatening to walk / cancel in this appointment
- Blocking a signature that was about to happen
- Safety / code / legal risk
- Outage or system-down for post-install customer

**NOT URGENT** (common misuse):
- "My rep wants to look smart in front of the customer" — still route, but normal priority
- Post-sale scheduling questions
- Curiosity questions with no deal at stake
- "What's our pricing on X" when no specific customer is present
